Marrakech - The Red City

Marrakech, with its red walls and historic medina, is a cultural treasure. Explore its lively souks and iconic landmarks. The city's palaces, gardens, and vibrant street life make it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking an authentic Moroccan experience. Visit Jemaa el-Fnaa square, Bahia Palace, Majorelle Garden, and the Koutoubia Mosque. The medina is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
