Fez - The Medieval City

Fez, with its UNESCO-listed Medina, is a journey back in time. Discover ancient architecture, vibrant markets, and traditional crafts. The city's labyrinthine streets and historic landmarks offer a glimpse into Morocco's rich cultural heritage. Fes is famous for the University of Al Quaraouiyine, considered the oldest continuously operating educational institution globally. The Bou Inania Madrasa stands as a masterpiece of Islamic architecture. The tanneries in Fes el Bali showcase traditional craftsmanship, offering a sensory-rich experience.
